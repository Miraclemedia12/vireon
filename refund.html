1. Overview

This Refund Policy outlines the terms for payments made on the Vireon platform.

2. Registration Fee

The one-time registration fee is non-refundable once payment has been processed and account access has been granted.

3. Requesting a Refund

If you believe a payment was made in error or you have not received access to the platform after payment, please contact our support team at support@vireonofficial.com.ng within 7 days of payment.

4. Refund Processing

Approved refunds will be processed within 5–10 business days to the original payment method used during registration.

5. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are explicitly designated as non-refundable under any operational status metrics:

  • Registration fees after account access has been granted
  • Any fees or points issued for activities already completed on the platform

6. Contact

For refund requests or questions about this policy, contact us at support@vireon-official.site or visit our corporate contact page.
